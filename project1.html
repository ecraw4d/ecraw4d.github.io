US Population Density

Interactive Leaflet Choropleth

Overview

This interactive web map visualizes population density across the United States using a choropleth technique. Data was sourced from the US Census Bureau.

Methodology

The map was built using the Leaflet.js library. I classified the population density data into distinct bins and assigned a sequential color scheme to highlight areas of high concentration (like the Northeast corridor) versus lower density regions.
